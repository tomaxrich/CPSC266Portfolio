FAQ

    • What If I can't Surf?
    • We can teach you!
    • How often do you guys go on trips?
    • We do two Major trips every year. One in the first semester where we go out to BC to shred Gnar, and a second in the spring semester where we road trip down to California to Shred Gnar!
    • Is there a facebook page?
    • Yes there is! Click here to find out more!
    • What if I don't have anyone to ski with?
    • If you post on the facebook page generally someone will respond and you can organize a outing from there!
    • What if I don't ski, board, or surf?
    • Join the club anyway! Make some friends! Come on the surf trip anyways, and have a great time in California!
    • Are the Members welcoming?
    • You won't find a friendlier group of people at the university!
    • How do I get more Involved?
    • Talk to One of our executive members about it. You can find there contact information on our Meet the Team page.
    • Do I have to commit very much time to this Club?
    • No, there is hardly any time commitment for this club just good people and good times!
